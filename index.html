Features

Settleasy helps you track shared expenses and calculate fair settlements between participants. Whether it's a shared apartment, a group trip, or any collaborative expense scenario - Settleasy makes it easy.

  • Create unlimited settlements with multiple participants
  • Track expenses and income with multiple currencies
  • Automatic balance calculation
  • Smart settlement payment suggestions
  • Complete privacy - all data stays on your device
